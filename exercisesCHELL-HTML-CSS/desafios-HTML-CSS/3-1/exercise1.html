Turbulence

We are going through turbulence
turbulence within our hearts
the same clouds that rock the planes
are the ones that rock us now
anguish, despair, anxiety and depression
those are the emotions that are inside the cloud
our hearts yearn
yearn for the day that these clouds will empty
and become just peace and joy.
